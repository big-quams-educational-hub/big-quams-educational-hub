Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for JAMB?
You can register online via the official JAMB website or visit accredited CBT centers for assistance.
What documents do I need for Post-UTME screening?
Usually your JAMB result slip, birth certificate, and academic transcripts. Check with your chosen institution for specifics.
How can I apply for scholarships?
Keep track of announcements on our site and apply early. We also provide personalized scholarship alerts.
Can Big Quams help with course selection?
Yes! We offer personalized counseling to help match your skills and interests with suitable courses.
